### Dissociating the Mechanisms of Stroboscopic Hallucinations: A Trait-Based Test in Synaesthesia

As part of the online component to be complete prior to the in-person
experiment, this repo includes:

-   Informed consent

-   Pre-screening to check eligibility

-   Phenomenological Control Scale (Lush et al., 2020)

-   Plymouth Sensory Imagery Questionnaire (Andrade et al., 2014)

--\> [**Test the
experiment**](https://RoisinSharma.github.io/SLSTraitBattery/experiment/index?exp=README)
\<--

References:

Andrade, J., May, J., Deeprose, C., Baugh, S.-J., & Ganis, G. (2014).
Assessing vividness of mental imagery: The plymouth sensory imagery
questionnaire. *British Journal of Psychology*, *105*(4), 547–563.
<https://doi.org/10.1111/bjop.12050>
